Mona Ezzat-Velinov's Bio

One Medical Group is an innovative, primary care doctor's office that offers health care designed around your needs. Our goal is to provide a superior level of service combined with a variety of unique member benefits which take the hassle out of seeing a doctor. These benefits include:

* Doctor appointments when you need them: same day & evening available
* Locations near where you live and work
* Doctor appointments that start on time
* Direct email access to your doctor

Plus, we make health care easier by accepting most PPO & HMO insurance plans. To support our practice, our members pay an annual membership fee of $149. Services offered include: primary care medicine, integrative medicine including acupuncture, nutrition counseling, in-house lab services, women's health and annual exams, men's health, physical exams, travel vaccinations and more!

Described by her patients as kind and knowledgeable, Mona listens attentively to her patients' health concerns and helps them establish a holistic plan to work toward optimal health. She has a particular interest in treating hyperglycemia, hyperlipidemia, obesity, and digestive disorders, emphasizing the importance of preventive care. Mona received her medical degree at Rush Medical College in Chicago and completed her internship and residency in family medicine at the University of Michigan. She is board-certified in Family Medicine and Integrative/Holistic Medicine.
